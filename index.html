Artist Statement

This website contains artwork I created myself which I am very proud of. You will find completed artwork from last semester and work in progress pieces from this semester. As an artist I want to have my artwork be a reflection of myself. You will notice various pieces using different mediums such as video, code, and sculpture. I frequently think about trying different mediums and techniques to broaden my horizons. I look forward to creating and adding new pieces to my gallery where people would be interested in seeing them. Why I picked these pieces for my portfolio is that each one carries a cherished memory that brings me back to when I was creating them. I like to think of my portfolio as a portfolio of memories.It lets me look back in time to see where I was and the progress I have made.
